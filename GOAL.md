# The Goal

Jacket is the spatial reasoning layer AI never had.

Any AI model — Claude, GPT, Grok, Ollama — can be wrapped with Jacket and gain the ability to think and output in real 3D space. The model doesn't change. The jacket gives it a spatial world model it never had: geometry, constraints, physics, printability.

The first demo: you describe something in plain English. Jacket produces a print-ready STL file. No CAD. No Blender. No 3D skills. Just describe it.

## The north star

A person who bought a 3D printer but can't design anything opens Jacket, types what they want, and prints it in an hour. They've never heard of OpenSCAD. They don't need to.

## What this is NOT

- Not a new AI model
- Not a chat UI
- Not another text-to-mesh model that produces unusable blobs
- Not locked to one AI provider

## Why it wins

The moat is the jacket itself: the spatial world model, the constraints engine, the geometry translation layer. Anyone can wrap an API. Nobody has built the layer underneath.

## Success looks like

- 10k+ GitHub stars
- Developers building adapters for models we didn't think of
- Normal people printing things they described in a sentence
- The phrase "put Jacket on it" meaning something
